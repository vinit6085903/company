Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services and process.

What is your typical project timeline?

Project timelines vary based on complexity and scope. A simple website may take 4-6 weeks, while a complex web application could take 3-6 months. We provide detailed timelines during the discovery phase and use agile methodologies to ensure timely delivery.

Do you provide ongoing maintenance and support?

Yes, we offer comprehensive maintenance and support packages that include regular updates, security patches, performance monitoring, and technical support. We believe in building long-term partnerships with our clients.

What technologies do you work with?

Our team is proficient in a wide range of technologies including React, Angular, Vue.js, Node.js, Python, Java, .NET, iOS, Android, React Native, Flutter, AWS, Azure, Google Cloud, and various AI/ML frameworks. We choose the right technology stack for each project based on requirements.

How do you ensure project quality?

We follow industry best practices, conduct code reviews, implement automated testing, and perform rigorous quality assurance at every stage. Our process includes unit testing, integration testing, security testing, and user acceptance testing before deployment.

Can you work with our existing development team?

Absolutely. We often collaborate with in-house teams, providing expertise in specific areas or augmenting existing teams with additional resources. We adapt our workflow to integrate seamlessly with your team and processes.
