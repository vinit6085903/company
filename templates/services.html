Common Questions

Contact FAQs

Find answers to frequently asked questions about contacting and working with us.

What is your typical response time?

We typically respond to all inquiries within 24 hours during business days. For urgent matters, please call our emergency support line at +91 9998887776, which is available 24/7 for critical technical support.

Do you offer free initial consultations?

Yes, we offer a complimentary 45-minute initial consultation for all new clients. During this session, we'll discuss your project requirements, business objectives, and provide preliminary recommendations. This helps us understand your needs and determine how we can best support your digital transformation.

What information should I include in my project inquiry?

For the most accurate assessment, please include: your project goals, target audience, preferred technologies (if any), budget range, timeline expectations, and any specific features or requirements. The more details you provide, the better we can understand your needs and provide relevant solutions.

Do you work with international clients?

Yes, we work with clients worldwide. We have extensive experience collaborating across different time zones and can accommodate various communication preferences. Our team is fluent in English and we have team members who speak Spanish, French, and Mandarin to better serve our global clientele.

How do you handle project confidentiality?

We take confidentiality and data security very seriously. Before discussing any project details, we're happy to sign a Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA). All client information and project details are kept strictly confidential, and we follow industry best practices for data security, including encryption, secure communications, and access controls.
